Welcome Class Of 2021!! Graduation of the current junior class is right around the corner! There are several requirements that all seniors must meet to successful transition from High School. ACT Prep Resources Testing and Education Reference Center This is an excellent FREE resource provided by the Tennessee Electronic Library, with three full length practice tests and detailed answer explanations. Additionally, you can take an online ACT Prep course, download Peterson's ACT Guide e-book, find test taking strategies, ACT word of the day, and more! You must create an account to access the material, but this allows you to return to the site as you wish to work! https://tntel.info/Students/High-School Kaplan Free Online ACT Practice Kaplan offers some FREE ACT Prep, including the ACT Pop Quiz, ACT Question of the Day, ACT 20-Minute Workout, and ACT Turbo Test. The ACT Question of the Day will e-mail you a message daily and includes detailed answers. You must sign up for an account to get the full access, and expect ads and emails encouraging you to purchase their test prep package. https://www.kaptest.com/act/free/act-practice Union Test Prep Union Test Prep is a FREE site offering practice tests, flash cards, study guides, and test taking tips. Work at your own pace anonymously, or create a free profile to save your progress. This site does have ads that can be distracting, but you have the option to turn them off for $5. https://uniontestprep.com/act ACT Test Prep Blog by Prep Scholar This blog post if full of great ACT information, including links to several ACT-provided paper tests you may take on paper and score yourself. (This is a great tool to see how the test will actually look on test day!) Additionally, find test taking tips, and links to other test prep sites.
