ABOUT US!

Welcome Madison Academic Class of 2021. At Madison Academic, we provide students access to a rigorous post-secondary preparatory program that inspires our students' intellectual curiosity. We are a national blue ribbon school (awarded in 2014) and have been named a state reward and/or progress school for the last 5 years. Your curriculum at Madison will include all honors, AP, or dual-enrollment courses, preparing you to succeed in whatever post-secondary opportunities you may pursue! Madison Academic is also a place to pursue any and every extracurricular activity. From Academic Decathlon to Fine arts, Madison Academic provides its students the means to prosper.
